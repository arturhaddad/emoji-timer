Privacy Policy - Emoji Timer

Last updated:

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Emoji Timer" extension collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information Collected

The Emoji Timer extension DOES NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All extension functionality operates locally in your browser.

2.1 Locally Stored Data

The extension may store the following information only in your local browser (using the Chrome Storage API):

  • Custom timer settings (time intervals and emojis)
  • Timer size preferences
  • Elapsed time of timers

This data remains exclusively on your device and is not shared with third parties.

3. Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To inject the timer into the active tab
  • scripting: To execute the timer code on the page
  • storage: To save your settings locally

None of these permissions are used to collect personal data or browsing information.

4. Use of Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no data to use, share, or sell. The extension operates entirely offline and locally.

5. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties, as we do not collect any personal information.

6. Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we implement appropriate security measures to protect any information stored locally in your browser.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The extension does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data stored locally by the extension
  • Modify or delete extension settings
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
